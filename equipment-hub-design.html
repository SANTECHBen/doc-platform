What does fault E-217 mean and how do I clear it?
Assistant

E-217 means the E-stop circuit has opened on the conveyor. It's a safety interlock — the drive will not restart until the circuit is physically re-closed and reset.1

To clear:

  1. Verify the equipment is safe to re-energize.
  2. Inspect each E-stop button on the perimeter for depression or damage.
  3. Pull the tripped button clockwise to reset it.
  4. Confirm the safety-relay LED on the control panel returns to solid green.2
  5. At the HMI, acknowledge the fault and press Reset → Start.
Sources
[1]
Fault E-217 troubleshooting Safety
"E-217 indicates the emergency-stop circuit has been opened. The drive is locked out by the safety relay and cannot be restarted remotely…"
[2]
Control panel indicator reference
"Safety-relay K1 shows solid green when all E-stops are released and the circuit is continuous."
