How it works

From scan to fix in three steps.

No new hardware. No new software for your team. Your existing documentation becomes a structured, searchable, audited resource.

Stick a QR on the machine

Branded QR labels printed and shipped. Place them next to nameplates, control panels, or access doors. Each code resolves server-side — re-targetable, revocable, audit-logged.

Tech scans with any phone

Native camera, no app to install. The PWA loads instantly and resolves to the asset's authored content pack — model, serial, content rev, install date, all in one screen.

Right answer, every time

Section-anchored manuals, parts lists with diagrams, structured procedures, AI assistant grounded on your published content. Safety-critical procedures quoted verbatim — never paraphrased.

Features

Built for technicians who don't have time to scroll.

Every interaction is scoped to a single physical asset. Authored once per OEM model, resolved per serial, audited per scan.

01 · Identification

One scan. The whole asset.

Brand-aware UI shows model code, serial, content revision, customer, install date, and any open work orders — before the tech even reads a manual. The asset is the home page; everything else flows from there.

  • Scoped to the exact serial — two identical machines at different sites can have different content versions
  • Branded per OEM with custom logos, colors, and accessibility-validated contrast
  • Server-side QR resolution: re-target on equipment swap, revoke on theft
02 · Documentation

Sectioned by part. Anchored to the page.

Manuals split into part-specific sections. Page ranges for PDFs, time codes for video, text excerpts for markdown. Authored once against an asset model — one source of truth per OEM doc, not 31 pre-split copies.

  • Page anchors with optional Y-crops for sub-page boundaries
  • Automatic re-validation when OEM publishes a manual revision
  • Procedures author-able as structured checklists with step-by-step evidence
03 · AI assistant

Plain English in. Cited answers out.

Ask anything about the equipment. Answers are grounded on the published content for that exact serial — every claim traces back to a section in the manuals. Safety-critical procedures are quoted verbatim, never paraphrased.

  • RAG retrieval over the asset's pinned content version
  • Refuses to summarize lockout/tagout — quotes the OEM directly
  • Source citations on every paragraph
04 · Procedure viewer

Open straight to the right page.

PDF sections render at the exact page range with safety warnings visually flagged. Pinch-to-zoom on phones, fullscreen on tablets. Every view is audit-logged with timestamp, user, and content revision — enterprise-grade compliance trail.

  • Native PDF rendering (pdfjs); no print-and-scan workflow
  • Safety-critical banner forces verbatim review before override
  • Scoped to the asset's pinned content version — frozen for audit
05 · Parts catalog

Every part, every diagram, one tap.

Searchable by part number, name, position, or cross-reference. Each part links to its install procedure, exploded views, and OEM-branded ordering — re-routing aftermarket parts revenue back to the OEM that built the equipment.

  • BOM hierarchy with parent-part drill-down
  • Cross-reference search for legacy part numbers
  • OEM-controlled ordering links — capture aftermarket leakage
06 · Issue reporting

Report from the floor in 30 seconds.

Severity, photos, attachments — issues route to maintenance with the asset, scan history, and content rev already attached. Phase 2 syncs work orders to your CMMS via webhook (Maximo, UpKeep, Fiix) so your existing system stays the source of truth.

  • Native camera capture with auto-attached EXIF metadata
  • Five severity levels with color-coded urgency
  • CMMS bidirectional sync on roadmap
07 · Field authoring

Capture tribal knowledge in real time.

Techs document procedures right at the equipment. Voice-to-title, step-by-step photo and video evidence, time-stamped on every action. Saved as unverified drafts; an admin promotes them to published content. Turn institutional knowledge into authored content without a paper checklist in sight.

  • Voice input for step titles and notes — gloved hands welcome
  • Auto-saved drafts survive battery death, app crash, lost signal
  • Admin promotion workflow keeps the field log honest
