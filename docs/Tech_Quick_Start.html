Equipment Hub — Tech Quick-Start

A one-page reference for technicians on the floor. Print, laminate, post near the equipment.

Step 1 — Find the QR sticker

Look for a square Equipment Hub sticker on the equipment. It’s usually near the nameplate, the control panel, or the access door. Each piece of equipment has its own sticker.

Step 2 — Scan it

Open the camera on any phone or tablet — iPhone, Android, no app to install. Point the camera at the sticker. A link appears at the top of the screen. Tap it.

Step 3 — Get what you need

The screen shows the equipment you scanned. Across the bottom, five tabs:

  • Docs — manuals, drawings, schematics, videos. Sectioned by part — scan a part QR and only the relevant pages show.
  • Parts — every part on this machine. Tap one to see drawings, replacement procedures, and part numbers.
  • Training — required training for this equipment. Lessons, quizzes, sign-offs.
  • Chat — ask the AI a question about this machine. Answers come from the manuals — safety-critical procedures are quoted verbatim.
  • Issues — see open work orders, or report a new one.

Got a problem? Report it

In the Issues tab, tap Report issue. Add a short title, severity, and a photo if you have one. The work order goes straight to your maintenance team.

Got feedback for the platform itself?

Tap the speech-bubble icon in the top-right of the screen at any time. Tell us what’s broken, what’s missing, or what’s confusing. The SANTECH beta team reads every submission.

Tips that matter on the floor

  • No signal? Most of what you’ve recently viewed still works. Reports get queued and submitted when signal returns.
  • Bright sunlight wrecking the screen? Tap the sun/moon icon (top-right) to switch to dark mode.
  • Found a step that’s wrong in the manual? Use the feedback button. We’ll route it to the OEM.
  • Photos for safety-critical work are required. The platform won’t let you mark a step done without them — that’s the audit trail.
