A Connected Worker Platform purpose-built for material handling and
industrial automation. Stick a code on every machine. Your technicians
scan, see authored content scoped to that exact serial, and resolve
issues without flipping through 700-page PDFs.

**Three things you get:**

- No app to install — runs in any phone or tablet browser
- Built for the shop floor — gloved hands, bright sunlight, harsh wifi

# How it works

From scan to fix in three steps. No new hardware, no new software for
your team — your existing documentation becomes a structured,
searchable, audited resource.

## 01 · Stick a QR on the machine

Branded QR labels printed and shipped. Place them next to nameplates,
control panels, or access doors. Each code resolves server-side —
re-targetable when equipment is replaced, revocable on theft, and
audit-logged on every scan.

## 02 · Tech scans with any phone

Native camera, no app to install. The PWA loads instantly and resolves
to the asset's authored content pack — model, serial, content rev,
install date, all in one screen.

## 03 · Right answer, every time

Section-anchored manuals, parts lists with diagrams, structured
procedures, AI assistant grounded on your published content.
Safety-critical procedures quoted verbatim — never paraphrased.

> **Why this matters:** MHI technicians spend 20–40% of their wrench
> time hunting for the right manual page. Equipment Hub collapses that
> to a single QR scan — and every interaction is audit-logged for
> compliance, training records, and procedure evidence.

# Features

## 01 · Identification

**One scan. The whole asset.**

Brand-aware UI shows model code, serial, content revision, customer,
install date, and any open work orders — before the tech even reads a
manual.

![Asset overview](example-1.jpg){width=2.5in}

- Scoped to the exact serial — two identical machines at different
  sites can have different content versions
- Branded per OEM with custom logos, colors, and accessibility-validated
  contrast
- Server-side QR resolution — re-target on equipment swap, revoke on
  theft

\newpage

## 02 · Documentation

**Sectioned by part. Anchored to the page.**

Manuals split into part-specific sections. Page ranges for PDFs, time
codes for video, text excerpts for markdown. Authored once against an
asset model.

![Sectioned documentation list](example-2.jpg){width=2.5in}

- Page anchors with optional Y-crops for sub-page boundaries
- Automatic re-validation when an OEM publishes a manual revision
- Procedures author-able as structured checklists with step-by-step
  evidence

\newpage

## 03 · AI Assistant

**An AI that knows your equipment.**

Ask anything about the equipment. Answers are grounded on the published
content for that exact serial — every claim traces back to a section in
the manuals. Safety-critical procedures are quoted verbatim, never
paraphrased.

![AI assistant grounded on the asset](example-3.jpg){width=2.5in}

- RAG retrieval over the asset's pinned content version
- Refuses to summarize lockout/tagout — quotes the OEM directly
- Source citations on every paragraph

\newpage

## 04 · Procedure Viewer

**Open straight to the right page.**

PDF sections render at the exact page range with safety warnings
visually flagged. Pinch-to-zoom on phones, fullscreen on tablets.
Every view is audit-logged with timestamp, user, and content revision.

![PDF section view with safety banner](example-4.jpg){width=2.5in}

- Native PDF rendering (pdfjs); no print-and-scan workflow
- Safety-critical banner forces verbatim review before override
- Scoped to the asset's pinned content version — frozen for audit

\newpage

## 05 · Parts Catalog

**Every part, every diagram, one tap.**

Searchable by part number, name, position, or cross-reference. Each
part links to its install procedure, exploded views, and OEM-branded
ordering — re-routing aftermarket parts revenue back to the OEM that
built the equipment.

![Parts list with search](example-5.jpg){width=2.5in}

- BOM hierarchy with parent-part drill-down
- Cross-reference search for legacy part numbers
- OEM-controlled ordering links — capture aftermarket leakage

\newpage

## 06 · Issue Reporting

**Report from the floor in 30 seconds.**

Severity, photos, attachments — issues route to maintenance with the
asset, scan history, and content rev already attached. Phase 2 syncs
work orders to your CMMS via webhook (Maximo, UpKeep, Fiix) so your
existing system stays the source of truth.

![Work order creation form](example-6.jpg){width=2.5in}

- Native camera capture with auto-attached EXIF metadata
- Five severity levels with color-coded urgency
- CMMS bidirectional sync on roadmap

\newpage

## 07 · Field Authoring

**Capture tribal knowledge in real time.**

Techs document procedures right at the equipment. Voice-to-title,
step-by-step photo and video evidence, time-stamped on every action.
Saved as *unverified* drafts; an admin promotes them to published
content. Turn institutional knowledge into authored content without a
paper checklist in sight.

![Procedure documentation start](example-7.jpg){width=2.2in}
![Procedure step capture](example-8.jpg){width=2.2in}

- Voice input for step titles and notes — gloved hands welcome
- Auto-saved drafts survive battery death, app crash, lost signal
- Admin promotion workflow keeps the field log honest
- Every step gets timestamped photo/video evidence
- Procedures become reusable training material on promotion
