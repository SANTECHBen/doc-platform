Connected Worker Platform for Material Handling & Industrial Automation

SANTECH · 2026

Stick a QR code on every piece of warehouse equipment. A tech scans it with any phone and instantly sees the right manual, the right parts list, the right procedure, and the right training for that exact machine — sectioned per part, scoped per serial, audited at every interaction. Three-sided: OEMs, integrators, and end-customers each pay for different value from the same physical QR.

Beta — Q3 2026
Five hand-selected MHI partners. SANTECH authors content, ships QR labels, runs weekly check-ins. ~$25k value, $0 to participate. Founding-member: 50% off list, locked 24 mo.

1Scan & Identify

One QR scan resolves the exact serial number. Brand-aware UI shows model, content revision, install date, and any open work orders — before the tech even reads a manual.

2Sectioned Documentation

Manuals split into part-specific sections (page ranges, time codes, safety-critical flags). Authored once per OEM model; resolves automatically per asset.

3AI Assistant, Safety-Guarded

Plain-English questions, answers grounded on the published manuals. Safety-critical procedures are quoted verbatim, never paraphrased — sources cited.

4Procedure Viewer

Opens directly to the right page range with safety warnings prominent. Scoped to the asset's pinned content version. Every view is audit-logged for compliance.

5Parts Catalog

Searchable by number, name, position, or cross-reference. Every part links to its install procedure, exploded views, and OEM-branded ordering.

6Report Issues

Open work orders from the floor with severity, photos, and attachments. Routes to maintenance immediately. CMMS webhook (Maximo, UpKeep, Fiix) on roadmap.

7Field Documentation Authoring

Capture procedures right on the floor. A tech tap-titles the procedure (or speaks it via voice), then walks through each step with photos and notes — every step gets timestamped evidence. Saved as unverified until an admin promotes it. Turns institutional knowledge into authored content without ever leaving the equipment.
