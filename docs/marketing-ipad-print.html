01 · Identification

The whole asset, one glance.

Brand-aware UI shows model code, serial, content revision, customer, install date, and any open work orders — laid out across the iPad's full canvas without scrolling.

  • Scoped to the exact serial — two identical machines at different sites can have different content versions
  • Branded per OEM with custom logos, colors, and accessibility-validated contrast

02 · Documentation

Sectioned by part. Anchored to the page.

Manuals split into part-specific sections. The iPad grid view shows section thumbnails, page ranges, and unverified field-procedure overlays at a glance.

  • Page anchors with optional Y-crops for sub-page boundaries
  • Automatic re-validation when OEM publishes a manual revision
  • Field-captured procedures show as unverified until an admin promotes them
03 · AI assistant

An AI that knows your equipment.

Plain-English questions, cited answers grounded on this asset's published content. Safety-critical procedures quoted verbatim. The iPad's wider chat surface makes long technical answers easy to scan.

  • RAG retrieval over the asset's pinned content version
  • Refuses to summarize lockout/tagout — quotes the OEM directly
  • Source citations on every paragraph
04 · Parts catalog

Every part, every diagram, one tap.

Searchable by part number, name, position, or cross-reference. Each part links to its install procedure, exploded views, and OEM-branded ordering — re-routing aftermarket revenue back to the OEM.

  • BOM hierarchy with parent-part drill-down
  • Cross-reference search for legacy part numbers
  • OEM-controlled ordering links — capture aftermarket leakage
05 · Issue reporting

Report from the line in 30 seconds.

Severity, photos, attachments — issues route to maintenance with the asset, scan history, and content rev attached. The iPad form keeps the asset header in view while the work-order is being filled.

  • Native camera capture with auto-attached EXIF metadata
  • Five severity levels with color-coded urgency
  • CMMS bidirectional sync (Maximo, UpKeep, Fiix) on roadmap
06 · Field authoring

Capture tribal knowledge in real time.

Techs document procedures right at the equipment. Voice-to-title, step-by-step photo and video evidence on a tablet keyboard, time-stamped on every action. Saved as unverified drafts; admin promotes them to published content.

  • Voice input for step titles and notes — gloved hands welcome
  • Auto-saved drafts survive battery death, app crash, lost signal
  • Admin promotion workflow keeps the field log honest
  • Procedures become reusable training material on promotion
